Hope Community Mbale Foundation

Striving to create empowering opportunities where disadvantaged young people from across the Mbale region feel safe, valued, and inspired to achieve their full potential through sports.

“So many children in our community grow up in poverty, lacking positive role models and safe places to play and learn. We are committed to changing this through the power of sport.”

Our Approach: Building Bridges Through Sport

We believe that sports can transcend boundaries. Our programs use football, athletics, and team activities to bring young people together, fostering discipline, teamwork, and leadership.

By creating local and international relationships through tournaments, exchanges, and partnerships, we open doors to grants and sponsorships. These opportunities allow us to expand our facilities, provide better equipment, and reach more children in need.

We invite individuals and organizations to join us in this mission—whether through sponsorship, volunteering, or spreading the word.
